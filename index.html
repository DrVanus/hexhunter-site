The Hunt

Kite the horde. Hex from range.

Your hexes fire on their own, auto-aiming into the dark — your one job is to keep moving. Weave through the swarm, funnel the horde into your bolts, and never let them close the gap.

  • One-thumb control — drag to move; your arsenal aims itself.
  • Chain-lightning & frost hexes arc across packs from across the screen.
  • Positioning is everything — get surrounded and the dark takes you.
The Hunters

Eight ways to face the dark.

Pick your hunter in the loadout, then earn the rest with essence. Every hero rewrites the run — a passive, a stat identity, and a starting weapon that sets the whole build in motion. Start with Vess; unlock the other seven.

  • 8 distinct hunters — from the balanced Huntress to the kill-hungry Revenant.
  • Each shifts the whole build with its own passive and starting weapon.
  • Unlock with essence earned from every run into the dark.

The Arsenal

Draft your build. Ascend your weapons.

Every level, choose one of three cards — a new weapon, an upgrade, or an item that bends the run your way. Reroll or banish what you don't want, then max a weapon with its paired passive to evolve it into an ascended form.

The Deep

Something guards the deep.

Push far enough into a biome and its warden wakes — the Plague Abbot, the Frost Lich, the Ember Titan. A health bar stretches across the top, the screen fills with death, and the horde never stops. Outlast it for the richest haul of the run.

  • A boss per biome — each one deadlier than the last.
  • Fight the boss and the horde at once — no breathing room.
  • Survive the kill for a surge of essence toward your next unlock.
